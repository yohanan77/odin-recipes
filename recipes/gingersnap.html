Delicously Chewy Spelt Gingersnap Cookies

With the sweetness of maple syrup and rich molasses, along with the zesty punch of ground ginger, and the warmth of cinnamon, our sprouted spelt flour gingersnap cookies are bursting with flavor! Scrumptious with a glass of oat milk or your favorite herbal tea, these cookies also make a wonderful eatable gift to share with family and friends.

Made with plant-based organic ingredients and sprouted whole grain flour, there is simply nothing better than indulging in a snack that is both irresistibly tasty and good for you.
